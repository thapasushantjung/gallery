Will Hautech.ai keep the patterns and prints of my items in the generations?
Our new Naomi model is designed to keep the patterns and prints of your items in the generations with high precision.

Be sure to use a clean, high-quality image—this allows the model to recognize and reproduce every detail of your item accurately.
2
2
Can Hautech.ai fix the same model for my generations?
At the moment, it’s not possible to fix the same model for all generations. However, by using the same prompt, you can generate models with a consistent appearance.

With our upcoming AI model release, you’ll be able to fix a specific model for your generations and even upload images of the desired model. Subscribe to our blog to stay informed about this exciting update - blog.hautech.ai.
